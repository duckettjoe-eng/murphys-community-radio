# Murphys Irish Pub Radio Station Package

Prepared for Murphys Irish Pub meeting on June 17, 2026.

## The Idea

Create a dedicated Murphys Irish Pub internet radio station powered by Murphys Community Radio and Live365: a branded station for pub atmosphere, local promotion, event energy, and community reach.

This is a bigger package than a normal underwriter placement. It gives the pub its own always-on station presence, while Murphys Community Radio handles the build, programming workflow, station setup, metadata discipline, and ongoing support.

## What Murphys Irish Pub Gets

- A dedicated Live365 station built around the pub’s brand, voice, and music policy.
- Dayparted programming: pub-friendly daytime music, event promotion, local announcements, and later-night energy when appropriate.
- Underwriter acknowledgements and station imaging produced for the pub.
- A web player that can live on the pub site, MCR site, QR codes, and social links.
- A station library and playlists that can be edited over time.
- Optional live-stream workflow for pub events, band nights, interviews, and community moments.

## Why This Helps

- The pub gets a media channel it owns and can promote every day.
- Customers can hear “the pub sound” before, during, and after a visit.
- Events get more runway: live music nights, food specials, sports/community events, and seasonal promotions can be promoted on-air.
- The station can deepen the pub’s role as a local culture hub instead of just buying ads.

## Live365 Platform Notes

Live365’s public pricing page currently lists these relevant monthly plans:

- Broadcast 3: $199/month, 7,000 total listening hours, 100 GB storage.
- Broadcast 4: $499/month, 10,000 total listening hours, 200 GB storage, TuneIn On Air included, mobile apps included, premium support.
- Broadcast 5: $999/month, 20,000 total listening hours, 500 GB storage, TuneIn On Air included, mobile apps included, VIP support.

Live365 notes that overages are billed at $0.05 per listened hour. Their pricing page also says listener licensing coverage is included for the USA through ASCAP, BMI, SESAC, GMR, AllTrack, Word Collections, and SoundExchange, with Mexico and Canada coverage also listed. Live365 specifically says coverage depends on accurate real-time metadata for each track played.

## Live Music Royalty Question

Important distinction for the meeting:

- Live365 licensing is designed for internet radio streaming to listeners.
- Venue live-music public-performance obligations can be a separate issue for the physical pub space.
- You mentioned Live365 discussed a live music add-on. I could not find public pricing for that add-on on Live365’s public site, so the safest meeting language is: “Live365 has told us there may be a live-music add-on path; we are confirming exact coverage and pricing before representing it as included.”

Recommended next question for Live365:

“For a bar/restaurant with live bands, does your live-music add-on cover only the online stream of live performances, or does it also satisfy the venue’s in-room public-performance obligations for ASCAP/BMI/SESAC/GMR/other PROs? What is the monthly price, what territories are covered, and what reporting is required?”

## Suggested Package Shape

Custom Station Partner: Murphys Irish Pub Radio

Included:

- Station setup and Live365 configuration.
- Pub-branded web/player page.
- Music library planning and dayparted clockwheels.
- Pub underwriting/imaging spots.
- Event promo rotation.
- Monthly programming refresh.
- Metadata hygiene for licensing/reporting.
- Optional live event streaming workflow after Live365 confirms coverage/pricing.

Suggested pricing discussion:

- Live365 platform cost should be passed through separately or built into the package.
- MCR service/production/programming fee should sit on top of platform cost.
- Start with Broadcast 3 for a controlled launch if the audience is mostly local and modest.
- Consider Broadcast 4 if they want stronger distribution features, mobile app inclusion, premium support, and a more “full station” posture.
